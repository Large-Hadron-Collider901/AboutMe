Top Ten Favorite Grounding Exercises

Over the past few years, I have adopted grounding exercises as a self-care protocol and they have been incredibly effective for me in relieving stress and anxiety. Grounding techniques are particularly helpful for people with Post Traumatic Stress Disorder, Dissociation, Panic Attacks or Anxiety. However, anyone can benefit from them. Grounding activities usually include using the five senses to become more aware of your surroundings and more present in the moment, which can help people disconnect from upsetting emotions or memories.
